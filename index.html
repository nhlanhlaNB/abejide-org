About Professor Abejide Ade-Ibijola

Google's #1 Ranked AI Professor in Africa

Global AI Leader & Innovation Strategist

Professor Abejide Ade-Ibijola is a globally recognised leader in Artificial Intelligence, innovation, and digital transformation. Currently a Full Professor of Artificial Intelligence & Applications at the University of Johannesburg, he is also a Distinguished Professor at Woxsen University (India) and University of Kigali (Rwanda).

Global Recognition & Leadership

  • Invited to speak at Oxford, Cornell, the US National Academies of Sciences, and Huawei TechWave
  • Advisor to the World Bank-sponsored ECOWAS Big Data Framework (2025), Ekiti State Innovation Council (Nigeria)
  • Founder of GRIT Lab Africa and Machines With Minds

Teaching Philosophy & Mentorship

"Tabula Rasa - every student is a blank slate capable of excellence."

Abejide's teaching is rooted in discipline, accessibility, and mentorship. He has supervised 65 postgraduate students to completion and directly mentored thousands across 26 African countries. His initiatives blend serious games, AI-driven classrooms, and VR/AR innovations, ensuring that learning translates into employability, entrepreneurship, and societal value.
